# Offer & Pricing

Purpose: the canonical offer ladder, prices, anchors, guarantees, and rules — reusable business context for the founder and AI agents. Anchor to these facts; never contradict them, never invent numbers.

## The Offer Ladder

Three rungs. The prospect can stop at any one. The Audit proves the work before anyone pays; the Build is the standalone purchase; the Concierge is an optional continuation chosen _after_ the Build — never a forced upsell.

| 1. The Audit             | Free                                                      | 20-min call where Donovin shows where the business leaks money + a written Money-Leak Map to keep |

### Rung 1 — The Audit (Free)

A 20-minute call where Donovin shows exactly where the business is leaking money — missed calls, cold quotes, dead follow-ups. Afterward he sends a written **Money-Leak Map**: biggest leaks ranked, plus the first fix he'd make. Theirs to keep whether they hire him or not.

### Rung 2 — The Build ($497 beta / reg. $1,494, one-time)

A one-time install. The client owns everything — every login and every line of code — and it keeps running the day they stop paying. Installs:

- 24/7 missed-call text-back + AI front desk
- Quote follow-up engine (recovers 20–35% of cold quotes)
- Automatic Google-review engine
- Past-customer reactivation
- Owner dashboard (calls answered, quotes recovered, hours saved)
- 30 days of tuning after handover
- The Money-Leak Map from the audit

**Note:** the beta price is set to rise toward the standard $1,494 as the beta closes. First clients get the discount in exchange for a before/after case study.

### Rung 3 — Operator OS Concierge ($750/mo founding rate)

Donovin stays on as the client's AI operator: biweekly build sessions (a new automation every ~2 weeks), on call between, and a running build log of everything shipped. The client still owns all of it. Month to month — cancel anytime and keep everything. **Chosen only after the Build — never a forced upsell.**

## The Value Stack & Real Anchors

The Build is anchored against two real, defensible comparables (sourced from Rockford-market pricing research — never inflate them):

- **Rented-agency anchor:** a GoHighLevel or marketing agency rents this same stack for **$797–$1,497/mo**, and you never own it. The Build is $497 once, owned outright.
- **Cost-of-problem anchor:** 3 missed calls a week at a $450 ticket is roughly **$5,400/mo** walking out the door. The fix is a one-time $497.
- Supporting stack comparables: answering services run $300–$800/mo; reputation/review tools run $50–$150/mo.

The Concierge is anchored against marketing agencies at **$2,200–$5,000/mo with a 12-month lock-in**. Operator OS is $750/mo, cancel anytime, and you own everything built.

## The Two Named Guarantees

- **The 10-Hour Guarantee (the Build):** if the Build doesn't give back 10 hours a week within 30 days, Donovin keeps working — free — until it does. (Step one, the audit, is free, so the risk is 20 minutes, not a dime.)
- **The Two-Week Guarantee (the Concierge):** if a working automation isn't shipped in the first two weeks, that month is free.

## Founding Scarcity (real)

**8 founding spots, 3 left.** This is real solo capacity, not manufactured urgency. Founding pricing (Build $497, Concierge $750/mo) locks for life while there's room. Update the "left" count as spots fill; never inflate it.

## Value-Equation Rationale

The ladder is built to maximize the value equation:

- **Dream outcome ↑:** stop losing jobs; get 10 hours a week back — more money, less chaos.
- **Perceived likelihood ↑:** the free audit proves it before they pay; the founder's enterprise-ops pedigree; the two guarantees; "you own it."
- **Time delay ↓:** free call now → Money-Leak Map next day → built in days.
- **Effort & sacrifice ↓:** done-with-you, installed in tools they already use, no contract, no learning curve.

**Pricing psychology:** $497 is a charm price that reads as value; $750 is a round-ish founding rate that signals a real relationship. The two anchors do the work — the cost of _not_ fixing it ($5,400/mo) and the rented-agency cost ($797–1,497/mo) — using real comparables only.

## Offer Rules (non-negotiable)

- **No hype.** Banned: game-changing, revolutionary, 10x, secret, unlock, supercharge, seamless, cutting-edge, AI-powered — and exclamation points. Plain, direct, loss-framed. Specificity over superlatives.
- **Never write "$X value" without a real, named comparable.** Specific numbers and real market comparables only; never inflate the anchors.
- **Never fabricate proof or scarcity.** The only real testimonial in hand is cross-industry (Shea S.) — frame it honestly, never disguise it as a trades business. Keep the "left" spot count and any Google rating honest and current.
- **The Concierge is never a forced upsell.** It's offered only after the Build, once the client has seen the work.
- **Ownership is the through-line.** No contracts, cancel anytime, you own everything (every login, every line of code) — the anti-agency edge, recurring-compatible.
