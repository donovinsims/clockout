# Messaging Guide

> **Last updated:** June 18, 2026
> **Brand voice:** Direct, no-fluff, confident. Outcome-focused. Zero jargon.

---

## Brand Voice

| Attribute | Style |
|-----------|-------|
| **Tone** | Direct, confident, conversational. No hype, no fluff. |
| **Style** | Speaks like an operator, not a marketer. Short sentences. Specific numbers over generic claims. |
| **Personality** | Honest, competent, local. Knows the trades because he's from here. Doesn't oversell — the guarantee does the talking. |
| **Voice inspiration** | A knowledgeable colleague who's seen how the big companies work and is telling you exactly what to fix. |

### Voice Rules
- **Use real numbers:** "62% of calls go unanswered" not "many calls are missed"
- **Name the leak in dollars:** "$5,400/month" not "significant revenue loss"
- **No corporate speak:** No "synergize," "leverage," "optimize," "holistic"
- **Be specific about time:** "48-hour audit" not "quick audit"
- **Show, don't tell:** Let the guarantee and the data make the case
- **Local language:** Name real towns (Rockford, Roscoe, Beloit), not "your area"
