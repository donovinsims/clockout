## Company Description

**One-liner:** Done-for-you automation for local service businesses.

**What it does:** I audit a trade shop's lead-to-cash workflow in 48 hours, identify every revenue leak (quantified in dollars), then build custom automations inside the tools they already use to stop those leaks. Full build takes 7 days. Total engagement: 9 days. The customer owns everything — every login, every API key, every doc, every line of code.

**Product category:** Business automation / workflow automation for home service trades.

**Product type:** Service (done-for-you automation builds). Not SaaS — one-time flat fee, customer-owned.

## Product Lines

### 1. Clockout (Core Offering)
Done-for-you automation builds for local service businesses. The main product — what the marketing site sells.

**Target:** Owner-operated trade shops (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, cleaning).
**Delivery:** 9 days (48-hour audit + 7-day build).
**Differentiation:** One-time price, customer owns everything, lives inside existing tools.

### 2. Operator OS
A personal productivity system for founders, operators, and managers. Three-pillar framework (Capture → Route → Surface) that ends the fire-drill cycle.

**Two delivery modes:**
- **For your shop:** Delivered as part of a Clockout build (included)
- **For you personally:** Standalone personal operating system for founders — inbox triage, calendar defense, follow-up cadence

**Status:** Early access / piloting with founding cohort.
