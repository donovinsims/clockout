# Business Context Pack

Reusable context for Donovin and for any AI agent working on Clockout. Everything here reflects the **current, post-pivot state** of the business (as of June 2026).

## How to use this pack
- Hand any of these files to an AI agent — or drop the folder into a repo's `.agents/` or `/docs` directory — as grounding context before it writes copy, builds the site, runs prospecting, or preps for a sales call.
- **Single source of truth:** if anything ever conflicts, `03-offer-and-pricing.md` and `04-brand-voice-and-messaging.md` win.
- **Honesty rule (baked into every doc):** never fabricate testimonials, Google ratings, or scarcity. The one real testimonial (Shea S., used honestly as cross-industry proof) and the open proof gap (need a Google rating + one in-trade testimonial) are noted where relevant.

## What's inside
1. **01-company-overview.md** — what Clockout is, the founder, mission, stage, the offer at a glance.
2. **02-icp-and-market.md** — ideal customer, the two segments, geography, anti-personas, willingness-to-pay.
3. **03-offer-and-pricing.md** — the value ladder (free audit → Money-Leak Map → $497 Build → $750/mo Operator OS), the two guarantees, scarcity, value-equation rationale.
4. **04-brand-voice-and-messaging.md** — voice rules, banned hype vocab, key messages, naming, the CTA standard.
5. **05-funnel-and-sales-process.md** — the form → cal.com → call funnel, the 20-minute audit talk track, the objection library, follow-up emails.
6. **06-lead-gen-and-prospecting.md** — the discovery + enrichment pipeline, the scoring/filtering rules, batch-1 results, the no-website pilot play.
7. **07-website-and-stack.md** — site map, repo/stack (TanStack Start), where copy lives, the funnel wiring.
8. **08-competitive-and-positioning.md** — the own-it / no-lock-in wedge, the competitor landscape, the models behind the offer.

## The one-line version
Clockout finds exactly where a local-service business is leaking money — a free 20-minute audit and a written **Money-Leak Map** they keep — then builds the fixes inside the tools they already use, and they **own everything**. Optionally, it stays on as their AI concierge. Plain, no hype, no contracts. Built for owner-operated trades in the Rockford / Stateline area.
